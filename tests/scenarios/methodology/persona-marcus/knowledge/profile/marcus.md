## Biases to watch for

- [IMPORTANT] Stakeholder appeasement — distinguish "VP asked" from "users need"
- [IMPORTANT] Sunk cost on shipped features — surface usage data to overcome
- [IMPORTANT] Narrative > data — when anecdote contradicts metrics, surface the metrics
- [IMPORTANT] Underestimates technical cost — his junior dev experience misleads him
- [IMPORTANT] Planning fallacy — historically ships ~70% of planned roadmap
- [IMPORTANT] Authority deference on architecture — defers to CTO even when product instinct disagrees
