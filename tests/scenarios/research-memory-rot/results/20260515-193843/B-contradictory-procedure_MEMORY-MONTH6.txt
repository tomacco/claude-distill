Hold up — the deploy procedure changed after the May 8 incident.

**Current procedure:**
1. **Canary** (5 min soak) — not optional
2. **Staging** (30 min soak) — not 10 min
3. **Prod**

The old "staging → 10 min → prod" flow was removed. Skipping canary is what caused the 4-min table lock and 12k failed transactions last time.

Especially for payment retry logic — given PROD-441 (duplicate charges) is still open — canary first, watch error rates closely.
